# Build a Business Case and Calculate ROI

## Purpose

When communicating with executives, you need to:

- **Clearly articulate** how the MVP aligns with key business objectives
- **Frame your communication** around pain points that resonate with leadership

## Business Case Components

### Alignment with Business Objectives

- Reducing time to market
- Fostering innovation
- Competitive advantage

### Address Leadership Pain Points

- Costs
- Slow development cycles
- Risks

### Well-Defined Plan

- MVP adoption strategy
- Rollout plan

### External Validation

- Industry reports (Gartner, etc.)
- Case studies highlighting platform engineering value
- Improved developer experience evidence

## Position the MVP

Frame it as a **core driver of value** for the organization to gain executive attention and secure buy-in.

## Calculating ROI

ROI calculation should focus on **quantifiable metrics** that executives understand:

### Costs to Consider

- **People costs** associated with platform team
- **Expenses of building the platform** (including tooling)
- Comparison to potential cost savings from alternative solutions

### Expected Returns

- **Performance improvements**
- **Direct cost savings**
- Reduced operational overhead
- Lower infrastructure costs

### Metrics to Leverage

- **Complexity index**
- **Onboarding time**
- **Service create time**
- Early successes from MVP phase

### Intangible Benefits

Consider including:

- **Talent retention** value
- Reduced attrition due to better developer experience

## Presentation

- Use **clear business language**
- Support with **simplified data and visualisations**
- Project **long-term impact** (e.g., over 3-5 years)
- Showcase **sustained value**

## The Goal

Construct a compelling business case firmly rooted in **strategic alignment** and supported by transparent, well-articulated ROI calculations to:

- Convey value proposition effectively
- Increase likelihood of securing necessary support and resources
- Focus on how MVP addresses key concerns and contributes to overall success

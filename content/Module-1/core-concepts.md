# Core Concepts and Foundations

## What is Platform Engineering?

Platform engineering is the discipline of designing and building toolchains and workflows that enable self-service capabilities for software engineering organizations in the cloud-native era.

**Source**: [Platform Engineering Blog](https://platformengineering.org/blog/what-is-platform-engineering)

## What is an Internal Developer Platform (IDP)?

An Internal Developer Platform (IDP) is built by a platform team to create golden paths and enable developer self-service.

**Source**: [Internal Developer Platform](https://internaldeveloperplatform.org/what-is-an-internal-developer-platform/)

## Key Takeaways

- Platform engineering focuses on improving developer experience
- IDPs enable self-service for application developers
- Golden paths provide standardized, optimized workflows

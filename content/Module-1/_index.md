## Key Definitions

**Platform Engineering**: The discipline of designing and building toolchains and workflows that enable self-service capabilities for software engineering organizations in the cloud-native era.

**Internal Developer Platform (IDP)**: Built by a platform team to create golden paths and enable developer self-service.
